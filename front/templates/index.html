About IGA HIFADHI

IGA HIFADHI is a Machine Learning-Powered Counseling Recommender System designed to provide personalized subject recommendations for O-Level and A-Level students in Kigali. Our solution aims to reduce dropout rates and align academic tracks with individual aptitudes and labor market trends.
